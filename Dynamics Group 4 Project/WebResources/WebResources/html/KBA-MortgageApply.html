Applying for a Mortgage

How to Apply for a Mortgage:

  1. Login to your account at Contoso Mortgages.
  2. Along the website's main menu bar located at the top of page, select Apply for Mortgage .
  3. Mortgage Fields:
    1. Name: The name can be anything you like that helps you reference the mortgage.
    2. Region: Specify the region in which you live.
    3. State: This field only appears if you selected 'US' as the region. If you live in the US, please specify which State you live in.
    4. Amount: Specify the mortgage amount in US Dollars.
    5. Term (Years): Specify the term of the mortgage, the options are 10, 15, 20, and 30 year mortgages.
